The Brillouin Zone

+ +


Welcome to the Brillouin Zone! With this site, you can investigate the relationship between electronic band structures, Brillouin zones, and crystal structures. After a simple process of selecting parameters with which to search through compounds from the AFLOW database, you will be directed to one of two pages.


The first page is the AFLOW entry page for your chosen crystral structure, where you can carry out a more thorough analysis of that structure and find all the details of the VASP calculation (what's VASP?) leading from the original structure to the final relaxed structure. On this page you will find an interactive three-dimensional model of the crystal structure that can be displayed in a variety of ways, including primitive cell, conventional cell, 20×20×20-angstrom block, and superimposed over its original structure with an indication of the RMSD between original and relaxed structures.


On the second page, you can interactively manipulate synchronized three-dimensional models of the crystal structure and its associated Brillouin zone. This page allows you to check the contributions of specific atoms, specific orbitals, and magnetic spin to the electronic band structure, as well as construct custom band structure diagrams.

To get started, select a Bravais lattice variation from the menu above.
